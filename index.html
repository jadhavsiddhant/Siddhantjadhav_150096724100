Black Panther

Black Panther, also known as T'Challa, is the king and protector of the fictional African nation of Wakanda. As one of Marvel’s most iconic heroes, he symbolizes strength, intelligence, and justice — blending tradition with futuristic innovation.

Why I Admire Him

  • He leads with compassion and wisdom, not just power.
  • He represents African excellence and pride on a global stage.
  • He stands for justice, unity, and doing what’s right — even when it’s hard.
